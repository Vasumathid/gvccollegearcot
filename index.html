Who We Are

A trust built on
disciplined teaching

GVC College of Education has run its B.Ed. degree programme since the 2007–2008 academic year, maintained by the GVC Educational Trust.

Our mentors focus on the habits that carry into a real classroom — observing closely, thinking in sequence, and turning theory into a lesson plan that works on a Monday morning. Every trainee leaves with the attitude, culture and values the profession asks for.

  • TNTEU-affiliated B.Ed. degree programme
  • Faculty mentorship built around practice teaching
  • Library, smart classrooms and dedicated methodology labs
