What Shapes A Graduate Here

Practice Teaching

Supervised classroom placements where lesson plans are tested in front of real students.

Communication Skills

Listening, speaking, reading and writing drilled as a teacher's working toolkit, not a formality.

Seats are filled as per TNTEU-prescribed counselling and reservation norms for aided/self-financing B.Ed. colleges in Tamil Nadu.

Internship runs across six Government and Government-Aided schools: Thiru G. Varatharajulu Chettiar Aided Hr. Sec. School and both Municipal High Schools in Arcot, Govt. Girls Hr. Sec. School (Arcot), Govt. Girls Hr. Sec. School (Walajapet), and Govt. High School (Kathiyavadi) — all within 5 km of campus. The programme runs 28 days each session with 7 dedicated mentor teachers, preceded by an orientation and a planning meeting with each school's head.
