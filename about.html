Our Story

Run by the GVC
Educational Trust

Only the B.Ed. degree course has been conducted here since the academic year 2007–2008 — a deliberate, single-programme focus rather than a wide, shallow prospectus.

That focus is the point: every part of the campus, faculty roster and academic calendar exists to prepare one kind of graduate — a teacher ready for a real classroom on day one.

Mission & Vision

What we are training
our students to become

Our Mission

Develop each student's knowledge, understanding, listening, speaking, reading and writing capacities into a working teaching practice.

Our Vision

To be a college whose graduates are recognisably skillful, sensitive to right attitude, and thorough in modern teaching method.

Our Values

Observation, logical thought, and the discipline to convert a teaching technique into a real classroom action.
